About Me.

With a robust business background and a dedicated focus on user experience, I am driven by a passion for solving problems in ways that are clear, simple, and elegant. As a Senior UX/UI designer, I believe that products must communicate effectively across all touchpoints—digital, physical, and spatial—as our interactions continue to evolve and change. My ultimate goal is to craft messages that are both cohesive and engaging.

My design process begins with extensive research and development, always putting myself in the end user's shoes. This empathetic approach allows me to approach projects playfully and thoughtfully, ensuring each design delivers a meaningful experience.

I hold a MA in Design from the University for the Creative Arts, and a MSc in Economics from the Mykolas Romeris University. I also have a Professional Diploma in UX Design from the UX Design Institute, accredited by Glasgow Caledonian University. My technical skills were further honed at 3W Academy, France, where I studied Web Development, and I have a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Communication Design from Vilnius College of Design.

Currently, I am a Senior Product Designer at Surfshark. Previously, I worked as UX/UI designer at Wire Salad, Telia, Danske Bank, and Visma, contributing to diverse projects that expanded my expertise. Since November 2017, I have also been sharing my knowledge as a UX/UI Lecturer at Code Academy.

Earlier in my career, I held various roles, including User Experience Designer at Kelkoo Group, Graphic Designer at COWI A/S, and Head of Marketing at Olympic Casino and Drogas (A.S. Watson Group). My journey started as a Market Analyst at Švyturys-Utenos Alus, part of Carlsberg A.S.

Each of these roles has honed my skills and broadened my understanding of user experience and design. I am committed to creating intuitive, user-friendly designs that meet and exceed user expectations.
